What platforms does Firetails support?

Firetails supports being installed on Windows, macOS, or Linux. It can be installed on Windows 7 or newer, and should work on most macOS and OSX versions. For Linux, it can be installed on most distros with a desktop environment.


Firetails runs on 32-bit and 64-bit architectures for Windows, x86_64 architectures for macOS and OSX, and x86_64 and armv7l architectures for Linux. In addition, you can also install Firetails on a Chromebook.
